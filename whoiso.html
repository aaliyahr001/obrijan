Who is Obrijan?

Obrijan originates from the great forestlands of Nebleuya, surrounded by fauna, flora, and beings who take roles dedicated to different parts of the Nebleuyan ecosystem. It is a special place because everything from the animals, plants, and planet exist together in perfect harmony and communication.
